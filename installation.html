Solar Installation Process

1. Site Assessment

Engineers inspect roof direction, shading, and structural strength to determine system size and placement.

2. System Design

Custom solar layout is designed based on energy needs and roof structure.

3. Mounting Structure Setup

Aluminum mounting frames are fixed securely to hold solar panels.

4. Panel Installation

Solar panels are placed and connected in series or parallel.

5. Inverter & Wiring

DC electricity from panels is connected to inverter to convert into usable AC power.

6. Grid Connection & Testing

System is tested for performance and connected to grid or battery storage.
